First article

An article is any member of a class of dedicated words that are used with noun phrases to mark the identifiability of the referents of the noun phrases.

Second article

The category of articles constitutes a part of speech. In English, both "the" and "a" are articles, which combine with a noun to form a noun phrase.

Third article

Articles are words that define a noun as specific or unspecific. Consider the following examples: After the long day, the cup of tea tasted particularly good.
